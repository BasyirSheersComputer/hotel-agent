BEST MONTHS TO VISIT:
• JUNE, JULY, AUGUST: Least rain, abundant sunshine, perfect beach weather
• APRIL, MAY, SEPTEMBER: Good weather with fewer crowds

MONTHS TO AVOID (IF YOU WANT DRY WEATHER):
• DECEMBER: Wettest month, heavy monsoon rains
• NOVEMBER, JANUARY, FEBRUARY: Significant rainfall
• MARCH: Transitioning out of monsoon

SURFING ENTHUSIASTS:
• NOVEMBER-MARCH: Best for surfing (consistent waves)
• Cherating International Surfing Competition typically in December
