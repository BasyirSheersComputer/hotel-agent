QUERY: Is parking free?
================================================================================
ANSWER: ### Parking Information:

- **Is parking free?**
  - Yes, parking at Club Med Cherating is free.
  
- **Parking Details:**
  - Open-air car park on-site with no charge.
  - Parking is on a first-come, first-served basis.
  - It is recommended to provide your car plate number in advance for smoother entry.
  - Upon arrival, drop off at the lobby to receive a parking coupon before proceeding to park.

- **For groups or multiple vehicles:**
  - Contact the resort in advance to arrange appropriate parking based on availability.
